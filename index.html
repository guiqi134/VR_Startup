Description

Our VR basketball arcade could give you some genuine practice and skills if you like shooting, and you don’t have to worry about all the unpredictable conditions in the real world. Or maybe, you’re just another person who enjoys the swish of basketballs falling into the net.

History

Actually, the idea of this project is proposed by Wenyou. In addition, most members in our group are fond of playing basketball. This project is the most suitable one to accomplish compared with other ideas we have discussed in the first day. It has a clear major goal, and doesn't involve lots of complex features which are hard to finish in one semester. And this is also the right time for our project to come out under the current COVID-19 situation, we offer those people who love playing basketball a chance to practice at home.

Features

Jedi Picking

We apply this magic picking strategy to make users more convenient. They don’t have to walk to the ball and do the pick action, when the trigger button is pressed, one of the balls will fly to your right hand just like the Jedi force.

Smooth Throwing

Instead of using a single frame to determine the force applied to the ball, we average several frames to apply a smooth throwing which won’t be sensitive to the tiny movement of the user like hand shaking.
