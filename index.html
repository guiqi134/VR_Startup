Videos

Create a 1-3 minute video announcement for your experience. Depending on the maturity of your project, you may frame it as a teaser, a trailer, or a feature highlight:
‣ Teaser Video: a short video intended to gain interest of a viewer in an attempt to build excitement about what will come next.
‣ Trailer Video: a commercial-like advertisement for an experience, that contains a series of selected clips from your experience, whether these be artificially created (in which you should indicate somewhere “NOT ACTUAL FOOTAGE”) or taken directly from your experience (in which no hedge is necessary).
‣ Feature Highlight: a “behind-the-scenes” kind of video that gives your users a detailed, guided tour of how your virtual reality experience looks and works, often narrated by a developer on the team. It’s not necessarily dramatically exciting, but instead focuses on specifics of your product in the most favorable light for your potential users or customers. (I think this one suits our project)

Logo & Icon

A logo/icon to represent your virtual experience. To put you in the right mindset, imagine what the logo/icon of your app would look like when running on Windows or Mac. If this is your first time designing an icon, consider (as inspiration or literally) using: The Noun Project (https://thenounproject.com/). Do bear in mind the licensing issues around icons if you decide to go that route. Please don’t copy and then not attribute. Please.
